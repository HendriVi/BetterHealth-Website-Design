Frequently asked questions

What to know before you begin.

BetterHealth is a physician-led medical practice. Psychiatric diagnosis, risk assessment, psychotherapy and medication remain part of care when indicated. Biomarkers, wearables and device-based methods are used to answer defined clinical questions. They do not replace clinical judgment.

BetterHealth is intended for adults whose symptoms are stable enough for planned outpatient work and who want a deeper assessment of persistent depression, anxiety, burnout, sleep or cognitive problems, reduced recovery, treatment resistance, post-concussive symptoms or prevention-related concerns. Suitability is assessed during the first call.

BetterHealth is a self-pay medical service and does not bill Swiss basic insurance. The listed fees cover the BetterHealth services described in each program.
