Frequently asked questions

What to know before you begin.

BetterHealth retains diagnosis, risk assessment, psychotherapy and medication when indicated. It adds a structured investigation of sleep, autonomic regulation, cognition, metabolic and endocrine health, inflammation, physical capacity, learning history and context. These findings are brought into one explanation and one coordinated plan.

The service is intended for adults whose symptoms are stable enough for planned outpatient work and who want a deeper assessment of persistent depression, anxiety, burnout, sleep or cognitive problems, reduced recovery, treatment resistance, post-concussive symptoms or prevention-related concerns. BetterHealth is not an emergency or crisis service.

You receive an integrated written report. It explains the symptom pattern, the leading biological and psychological hypotheses, the meaning and limitations of the measurements, the priorities for treatment and the questions that remain open. The aim is to make complex information usable without creating false certainty.
