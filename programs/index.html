Programs and prices

The same clinical method, with different levels of assessment and support.

Precision Psychiatry Assessment: CHF 6,000

For people with persistent symptoms, partial answers or several disconnected reports. We reconstruct the clinical pattern, define the most plausible contributing mechanisms and select measurements that can clarify the case. You receive an integrated report explaining what the findings mean, what remains uncertain and which next steps deserve priority.

Includes:

  • ✓ Extended psychiatric and medical assessment
  • ✓ Review of previous treatment, laboratory data and wearables
  • ✓ Targeted tests linked to the leading clinical questions
  • ✓ Written interpretation with priorities and practical next steps

Six-Month Integrated Treatment: CHF 15,000

For people who want BetterHealth to carry the assessment into treatment. We turn the working explanation into a sequenced plan, coordinate the relevant parts of care and review the response over six months. Psychiatric care, psychotherapy and physiological interventions are combined only when each component has a clear purpose in the case.

May include:

  • ✓ Everything included in the Precision Psychiatry Assessment
  • ✓ Physician-led sequencing and medication review
  • ✓ Psychotherapy and behavior change linked to the case
  • ✓ Sleep, nutrition, exercise, HRV biofeedback and selected modalities when indicated
  • ✓ Planned clinical reviews and repeat measurement where useful

Brain Health & Prevention: Individual quotation

For cases that require a wider diagnostic field or longer oversight, including treatment resistance, cognitive change, post-concussive symptoms, strong family risk or prevention under sustained professional demand. The scope may include extended cognitive or neurological assessment, coordination with external specialists and longitudinal retesting. Scope and pricing are defined after the initial clinical discussion.
