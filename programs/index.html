Programs and prices

One clinical method at three levels of depth.

Biomarker Deep Dive — CHF 6,000

For people who want to understand what may be driving their symptoms before deciding on a longer treatment pathway. The result is a ranked clinical formulation rather than a collection of disconnected test results.

Includes:

  • ✓ Clinical intake, symptom analysis and diagnostic orientation
  • ✓ Ranked biological and psychological system hypotheses
  • ✓ Targeted biomarkers and interpretation of existing data
  • ✓ Written case report and practical next steps

Six-Month Treatment Program — CHF 15,000

For people who want BetterHealth to turn the assessment into a coordinated treatment process rather than leaving them to integrate the recommendations alone.

May include:

  • ✓ Everything in the Biomarker Deep Dive
  • ✓ Personalized physician-led treatment plan
  • ✓ Psychotherapy, psychiatric care, sleep, physiology and behavior work as indicated
  • ✓ Nutrition, exercise and selected modalities matched to a defined target
  • ✓ Repeated measurement, progress review and adaptation

Brain & Prevention Program — Individual quotation

For complex, treatment-resistant, post-concussive or prevention-focused cases requiring extended diagnostics, multidisciplinary coordination, cognitive-health planning or longer-term follow-up.
