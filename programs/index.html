Programs and prices

Choose assessment, guided treatment or longer-term clinical oversight.

Biomarker Deep Dive: CHF 6,000

This assessment is for people who have persistent symptoms, partial answers or scattered results and want a coherent explanation before committing to longer treatment. We integrate the clinical history with selected measurements and produce a ranked formulation of what is most likely contributing, what remains uncertain and what should be addressed first.

Includes:

  • ✓ Extended psychiatric and medical intake
  • ✓ Review of previous treatment, laboratory data and wearables
  • ✓ Targeted biomarker testing selected from the clinical hypotheses
  • ✓ Integrated written formulation with priorities, limitations and next steps

Six-Month Treatment Program: CHF 15,000

This program is for people who do not want the assessment to remain a report. BetterHealth translates the formulation into a sequenced treatment plan, coordinates the relevant parts of care and follows the response over six months. Treatment may combine psychiatric care, psychotherapy and physiological interventions, but each component must have a defined clinical reason.

May include:

  • ✓ Everything included in the Biomarker Deep Dive
  • ✓ Physician-led treatment sequencing and medication review
  • ✓ Psychotherapy and behavior change linked to the formulation
  • ✓ Sleep, nutrition, exercise, HRV biofeedback and selected modalities when indicated
  • ✓ Repeat measurement and planned clinical reviews

Brain & Prevention Program: Individual quotation

This pathway is designed for cases that require a wider diagnostic field or longer oversight, including treatment resistance, cognitive change, post-concussive symptoms, strong family risk or executive prevention. The scope may include extended cognitive or neurological assessment, coordination with outside specialists and longitudinal retesting. Scope and pricing are defined after the initial clinical discussion.
