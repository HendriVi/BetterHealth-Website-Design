Detailed program information

Each program is customized to your needs.

Biomarker Deep Dive – CHF 6,000

A focused assessment for people who want to understand the biological and physiological factors influencing mood, energy, sleep, cognition, and long-term brain health. The exact testing is individualized based on your presentation and history.

May include:

  • ✓ Detailed medical and psychiatric history
  • ✓ Blood biomarker panel (metabolic, inflammatory, nutritional)
  • ✓ Sleep and circadian assessment
  • ✓ Wearable data interpretation
  • ✓ Integrated interpretation and recommendations

Four-Month Treatment Program – CHF 15,000

Assessment followed by four months of integrated treatment, including nutrition guidance, sleep optimization, exercise programming, and psychiatric or psychological support as clinically indicated.

Includes:

  • ✓ Full initial assessment
  • ✓ Physician-guided treatment plan
  • ✓ Nutrition protocol and supplementation guidance
  • ✓ Sleep and circadian optimization
  • ✓ Repeat measurements at 2 months and 4 months
  • ✓ Ongoing physician contact and treatment adjustment

Advanced Bespoke Program – Individual Quotation

For complex presentations requiring extended assessment, specialist coordination, advanced diagnostics, or longer-term follow-up. Cost is determined after an initial discussion.
