The brain forecasts the energy each moment will need. We measure whether the fuel arrives.

The premise

Insulin resistance, inflammation and disrupted cellular energy quietly narrow the fuel supply the brain depends on.

Metabolic brain health treats those root causes rather than symptoms alone, which is why the assessment begins with measurement, not a questionnaire.

Clinical method

One integrated approach.

Each step depends on the one before it. We measure, interpret, intervene, then measure again.

01 / Measure

Read the system

Comprehensive assessment of your symptoms, history, and biological systems using clinical interview and targeted testing.

02 / Interpret

Find the drivers

A physician analyzes your results to identify the metabolic, inflammatory, and physiological patterns most relevant to your presentation.

03 / Intervene

Nutrition as medicine

Implementation of a prescribed, biomarker-guided treatment plan using nutrition, sleep, exercise, and lifestyle modification.

04 / Optimize

Measure again

Retest your markers, track your progress, and adjust the plan based on what the data and your experience show.
