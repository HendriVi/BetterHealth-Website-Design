How we assess

Measurement guided by clinical thinking.

We do not order every test available. Instead, we select measurements based on your presentation and history, with the goal of building a coherent understanding that informs treatment.

Assessment domains

Clinical & psychiatric

Detailed history, current symptoms, functional impact, psychiatric diagnosis, risk assessment, medication review.

Blood biomarkers

Metabolic, inflammatory, nutritional, endocrine, and hematological markers, selected based on clinical indication.

Sleep & circadian

Sleep history, quality, timing, continuity, and any breathing concerns. Wearable data where available.

Metabolic & physical

Glucose regulation, body composition, cardiovascular fitness, activity patterns, and recovery.

Cognitive & neuro

Attention, memory, processing speed, executive function, and any neurological symptoms.

Autonomic regulation

Heart rate variability, blood pressure, recovery, breathlessness on exertion, and perceived stress tolerance.
